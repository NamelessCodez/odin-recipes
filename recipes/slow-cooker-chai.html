Slow Cooker Chai

Description

Easy version of chai tea, using whole spices and sweetened condensed milk. To vary the flavor, try adding a bit of star anise, fennel, allspice, vanilla bean, or nutmeg. For a sweeter tea, stir in a bit of brown sugar.

Ingredients

  • 3Â½ quarts water
  • 15 slices fresh ginger, peeled
  • 15 green cardamom pods, split open and seeded
  • 25 whole cloves
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 whole black peppercorns
  • 8 black tea bags
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Pour water into the crock of a slow cooker. Stir in the ginger, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and peppercorns. Turn to High; simmer for 8 hours.n
  2. Steep tea bags in the hot spiced water for 5 minutes. Strain tea into a clean container. Stir in sweetened condensed milk; serve hot.n
